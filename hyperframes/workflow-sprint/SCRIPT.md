## Shape language (no text on tiles)

- **15 abstract tiles** = the workflow's steps. Scattered and slightly rotated
  at first (the stuck mess — one tile visibly sags); alignment = mapping;
  three rows = sorting; a copper edge + drawn check on three tiles = the three
  automations "clicking into place".
- **The human row stays copper-warm** from the sort onward — human work is
  not eliminated, it is named and protected.
- **The gate beat** reuses the office-assistant film's approval motif (filled
  copper node + white ✓) so the site's films share one approval language.

## Honesty rules (non-negotiable)

- Tiles carry no labels — no invented step names, tools, or metrics.
- Exactly three automation ticks — the offer promises three, the film shows
  three.
- Both locales carry the same story with their own approved copy.
